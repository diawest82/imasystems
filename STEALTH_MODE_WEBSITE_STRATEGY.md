## VISUAL IDENTITY (Stealth Edition)

### Color Palette - Premium & Mysterious

```
Primary: Deep Charcoal #0f0f0f - Luxury, premium, secretive
Secondary: Metallic Silver #c0c0c0 - Sophisticated, refined
Accent: Muted Sage #6b8e7f - Calming, trustworthy (not technical)
Highlight: Cream #f5f1e8 - Premium, warm, elegant
Background: Off-white #fafbf8 - Luxury, minimal
```

**Rationale:**
- No blues (too technical/startup-y)
- No bold colors (suggests innovation)
- Metallics = premium, refined, luxury brand
- Muted tones = trustworthy, established
- No "quantum cyan" or tech indicators

### Typography - Premium & Established

**Headlines**: **Playfair Display** (serif)
- Luxury fashion brand aesthetic
- Elegant, timeless, sophisticated
- Suggests established expertise
- Reveals nothing technical

**Body**: **Lato** or **Poppins**
- Modern, readable, premium
- Clean, professional, approachable
- Not too minimal, not trendy

**Accent**: **Montserrat** (light)
- Elegant, refined spacing
- Used for quotes, callouts, subtle details

- Creates luxury brand feel

**Silver (#c0c0c0)**

**Sage (#6b8e7f)**
- Secondary accent color
- Trust and stability
- Calming, professional

**Cream (#f5f1e8)**
- Text on dark backgrounds
- Warm, elegant, readable
- Premium feel

**Off-white (#fafbf8)**
- Bright backgrounds
- Minimal, clean, professional

- Generous whitespace (not cramped)

- Elegant dividers (thin, sophisticated)
- Max-width 1200px (premium, not full-bleed chaos)
